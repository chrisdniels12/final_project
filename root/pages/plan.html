Plan

Resources to get started

Suggestions:

  • Plan early: Start planning early to find the best rates and reduce travel stress.
  • Create a budget: Create a budget that includes hotel costs, fuel costs, sightseeing fees, and food costs.
  • Plan your route: Plan your route in advance to avoid getting lost and to make necessary pit stops.
  • Select a destination: Choose a destination that is not too far, especially if you're traveling for the first time.
  • Prepare your car: Check your car's fluid levels, tire tread, tire pressure, wipers, gas levels, and lights. Pack an emergency car kit.
  • Make a packing checklist: Prepare a checklist of all the items you need to pack one or two weeks before your trip.
  • Plan your rest stops: Plan your rest stops and take breaks.
  • Stay hydrated: Bring healthy snacks and keep passengers entertained.

We can help!

Roadside America is here for you. Check out our explore page if you need ideas. Or if you're ready to start planning, click on the My Trip tab. Happy trails!
